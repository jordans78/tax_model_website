Tax Modeling Toolkit

Section 2 Overview of the Modeling Framework

The modeling framework is organized around a baseline scenario and one or more reform scenarios.

The general workflow is:

  1. Import raw data.
  2. Clean and structure the data.
  3. Define tax policy parameters.
  4. Estimate baseline liability.
  5. Simulate reform scenarios.
  6. Compare baseline and reform results.
  7. Present revenue and distributional effects.
